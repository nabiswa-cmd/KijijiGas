# Kijiji Gas System – Smart LPG Supply & Community Energy Platform

Kijiji Gas System is an innovative web-based platform designed to digitalize and streamline how LPG (cooking gas) is supplied, sold, managed, and delivered within local communities. The system connects customers, suppliers, and delivery agents into one intelligent ecosystem that makes access to clean and affordable energy easier, safer, and more efficient.

## Features

### For Customers
- Smart supplier locator to find the nearest trusted gas suppliers
- View prices and cylinder availability for different gas brands and sizes
- Place orders directly through the platform
- Track orders from request to delivery
- Secure and fast mobile payments (e.g., M-Pesa)

### For Gas Suppliers
- Stock tracking (filled & empty cylinders)
- Sales management and prepaid orders
- Multi-branch operations and supplier accounts
- Delivery assignments and tracking
- Real-time dashboards for profits, stock movements, and customer trends

## Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) Alignment
- **Affordable and Clean Energy:** Promotes safer LPG distribution and makes clean cooking energy more accessible at the village (“kijiji”) level.
- **Industry, Innovation & Infrastructure:** Empowers small and medium gas businesses to digitalize operations and deliver better service.

Kijiji Gas System creates a modern, transparent, and community-focused LPG supply network—bringing clean energy closer to every home.
